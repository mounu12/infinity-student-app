General Instructions

  1. Total duration of this test is 180 minutes.
  2. There will be 180 Single Option Correct Multiple Choice Questions.
  3. There are 3 sections in the question paper consisting of 45 questions of Physics, 45 questions of Chemistry and 90 questions of Biology
  4. Each question is allotted 4 (Four) marks for correct response.
  5. 1 (One) mark will be deducted for indicating incorrect response for each question.
  6. No marks will be deducted for not attempting a question.

Test Taking Instructions

  1. The countdown timer in the top right corner of screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination.
  2. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end by itself. You will not be required to end or submit your examination.
  3. The Questions Palette displayed on the right side of screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols:
    • You have NOT Answered the question.
    • You have Answered the question.
    • You have NOT Visited the question.
    • Answered and Marked for Review. These questions will be considered for evaluation.
    • Not Answered and Marked for Review. These questions will NOT be considered for evaluation.
    • To view instructions during the test, click on this icon
    • To see a comprehensive list of your questions during the test, click on this icon
  4. You can click on the "icon" which appears to the left of question palette to collapse the question palette thereby maximizing the question window. To view the question palette again, you can click on "icon" which appears on the right side of question window.
  5. Click on the question number in the Question Palette at the right of your screen to go to that numbered question directly. Note that using this option does NOT save your answer to the current question.
  6. Click on Save & Next to save your answer for the current question and then go to the next question.
  7. Click on Mark for Review & Next to save your answer for the current question, mark it for review for later, and then go to the next question.
  8. To DESELECT your chosen answer, click on the button of the chosen option again.
  9. Click on the “Answered”, “Not Answered”, “Answered and Marked for Review” and “NOT Answered and Marked for Review” tiles on the Question palette to view the relevant question numbers subject wise.
  10. Once you have finished taking the examination, please click on the “Submit” button.
  11. For receiving an advanced test feedback analysis make sure you:
    • Attempt at least 10% of the total questions in the test.
    • Spend at least 10% of the total test time.
